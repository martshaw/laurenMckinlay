Men’s Autumn/Winter

Men’s lace-up boot ‘Sergio’ features full brogue detailing as well as two different leather textures; smooth and embossed, creating a subtle black on black twist. This calf leather boot is a failsafe for the colder seasons with a full rubber sole to protect you against the wicked elements. Lace hooks allow you an extra tight fit around the ankle to always stay warm. Team yours with dark jeans and knitted top for a laid-back weekend look. ‘

Nando Marrone’ is a softer shade to that of the original black ‘Nando’; however both has the same elegant shape. This derby ankle boot is crafted from calf leather and comes ready equipped for the season ahead with an anti-slip coating added to a classic leather sole. ‘Nando Marrone’ is a perfect partner to keep you looking sharp at work or in your free time. Pair yours with a dark, tailored outfit for maximum appeal. 

Classical Chelsea boot ‘Ridolfo’ is an ideal style to take you right through the season ahead. Handcrafted from brown suede with brown elastic stretch you will have no problem pairing ‘Ridolfo’ with any casual outfit. Wear with your favourite jeans or spice things up with corduroys; a new wardrobe essential for men. The original leather sole has the addition of an anti-slip rubber coating for the colder months. 

‘Cristoforo’ is a black lace-up boot with a difference; the basic shape is taken from a classic Oxford shoe and extended at the ankle. Now you can have the all the elegance of the Oxford with all the benefits of a winter boot. The leather sole comes ready equipped for the cold and wet streets with a coating of anti-slip rubber. Crafted from suede and calf leather it is an ideal option for men’s business wear. Try pairing your ‘Cristoforo’ boot with tailored trousers and a simple crisp shirt. 

‘Emiliano’ is a charming boot for the man who wants to look sophisticated and crisp. Deriving out of the classic Oxford shoe our Emiliano boot is refined yet convenient to wear during the colder months. Our original leather sole has been adapted for the colder months with an anti-slip rubber coating. The warm brown tone of the suede and calf leather is a great combination for grey or brown tailored trousers.

Men’s Classic Shoes 

One of our most popular men’s shoe designs ‘Febris’ is black calf leather Oxford featuring semi- or half brogue details. Decorative perforations can be seen on the toe cap as well as along the curved line of the lace eyelets. Handcrafted for exceptional levels of comfort this handsome design will last you for years to come. Team with your best dark suit for an undeniably refined look. 

Crafted from black calf leather our ‘Federigo‘ Oxford shoe for men is undoubtedly a timeless classic. It features a half brogue effect with decorative perforations added over the toe cap and around the edge of the vamp. Five rows of lace eyelets help to shape its overall sleek and elegant appearance. Invest in ‘Federigo’ and you will have a shoe for every occasion, whether business or play. 

With intricate full brogue design and double-stitched cap toe, the elegant profile of our 'No. 3' Oxford will complement any business outfit perfectly. Traditionally crafted from black calf leather it makes a sound investment for the businessman who always aims to look his best. 

Full brogue Oxford ‘Philip’ is an immaculately detailed men’s shoe characterized by its traditional wingtip toecap. Handcrafted from black calf leather this classic design will keep you in style for years to come. The versatility of this style means it can be paired with a business suit or dress down in dark casual jeans, either looks equally chic. 

Men’s Oxford ‘Mariano’ is a classic full brogue shoe made from brown calf leather. It features the quintessential wingtip toecap with decorative perforations. Ideal to wear with tweed or grey and blue tones, this style can be worn for work as well as free time. 

Our ‘Guccio’ men’s Oxford is an extravagant full brogue bursting with character. The sleek profile is emphasized thanks to five rows of lacing, which also allow you a tight and comfortable wear. The light warm colouring of the cognac leather makes it a great partner for beige chinos and denim. Show yours off at the weekend in your favourite smart-casual outfit. 

Captoe Oxford ‘Bettino’ is an understated classic that is sure to see you through any smart or casual situation. The clean silhouette is formed close to the shape of the foot thanks to the straight-lined closed lacing. Crafted from black calf leather this elegant shoe is a wardrobe essential for any gentleman.

‘Jerome’ is a cap toe Oxford with modern cognac colouring, handcrafted from calf leather. A classic sleek shape is formed as a result of six-tier closed lacing. Refined yet restrained this style is just as handsome worn in or out of an office environment. Pair with grey or blue tailored trousers, or combine with jeans and a woollen jumper for an effortless chic look. 

Gloves 

Men‘s leather gloves ‘Bormio’ may at first seem straightforward and classical but further inspection shows they have a touch of the unexpected. Electric blue stitching pops against the black calf leather. An inviting choice, Bormio will pull together your winter business look. Lined with 100% cashmere.

 ‘Pinzolo’ is a traditionally handmade men’s glove in brown calf leather, lined with 100% cashmere. The soft, breathable leather will allow you to keep warm not just this winter but also many more to come. Invest in Pinzolo and stay fashionable as well as protected. For this season’s look, pair yours with a navy or racing green scarf. 

Wrap up warm in ‘Carona’ our women’s gloves in bordeaux. Handcrafted from calf leather and lined with wool for a rich and soft feel. A great colour for the season ahead and can easily be match with shoes, handbags and scarfs. 

Our women’s gloves ‘Cortina’ add a splash of character to any outfit thanks to the two-tone design. Seemingly black at first, a hint of cognac has been added to the wrist. Handcrafted from calf leather and lined with 100% wool you are sure to stay warm this season in your very own pair. Team with an all-black outfit for that refined look with an element of surprise.
