Hulger
Lovingly made designs for life

Innovative brand Hulger injects timeless grace and glamour into 21st century technology. Now manufacturing their own designs, initially the group upcycled nostalgic handsets to be used via cell phones. Once operating under the name ‘Pokia’, a certain communication giant soon coerced the London based team to rename.

Meet the international award winning Plumen 001 - your luminous, new household beaux. Saving up to 80% on energy bills and lasting approximately 8 times longer than a common light bulb, he’s more than just a pretty face. Created to put the humble light bulb on centre stage, the no-frills design is simple yet stunning. This collaboration between British talent Samuel Wilkinson and London based design brand Hulger has resulted in rapid popularity since it’s 2010 launch.

More Details Brand: Hulger / Samuel Wilkinson. Origin: UK. Material: Glass. Dimensions: 192 x 100 x 91 mm. Available in Bayonet Cap (B22) and Screw Cap (E26 or E27). 11 Watts. Two versions purchasable: the 220v to be used throughout most European countries and the 120v for outside Europe. If in any doubt please contact us on: service@fab.com or check www.plumen.com for details.

Fritz Hansen
Sleek and sublime Scandinavian design

Hej and welcome to the home of Contemporary Classics. If you thought Scandinavian design was all about Allen Keys or assembly instructions, then you’ve been missing out! Together with world-renown architects and designers, Fritz Hansen has produced more iconic chairs than you can shake a Lingonberry-dowsed stick at! 

Ant Chair is a stackable dining chair with an ménage-a-trois twist! The unconventional 3-legged piece was a design breakthrough for the granddaddy of Danish design Arne Jacobsen. Created in 1952 for the eversmaller spaces of new housing, the lightweight solution remains a prominent icon today. The svelte silhouette is made from 9 layers of bent beech veneer and comes in a wide range of colours and finishes. A standard 4-leg version is also widely available. 

More Details Designer: Designer: Arne Jacobsen 1952. Origin: Denmark. Seat height: 44cm, width: 51cm, depth: 48cm. Beech veneer seat and chrome base. 3 LEGS. No assembly required.

Rosita Bonita 
Full of Fanciful Fables 

Every piece of Rosita Bonita jewellery begins its life not on a workshop bench but in the etchings of a handmade illustration. Motifs are created of fairy tale, and legend. Her ideas are plucked from heavens and oceans, and tumble through bygone eras. Leathers, embossing, bold colours; overall giddy pieces with gusto! 

This winged Horseshoe Necklace is bound to be soring high in many a girl’s wish list. A golden foil-embossed horseshoe adorns a pair of granite and gold outstretched wings, on this gleaming statement piece. The emblem is screen printed onto a single piece of leather, which is attached to a short goldplated chain. Ideal for everyday wear with jeans and t-shirt, or could just as easily add some Glam rock to classic eveningwear! 

More Details Designer: Rosita Bonita. Origin: UK. Materials: leather, gold plated brass chain. Leather Plate: 14.5cm wide x 3-4mm thick. Features: Belcher and Bar chain, Lobster fastening clasp, heart shaped tab.
